SalaryLens shows you where any job offer falls in the market, right on the job listing page. No guesswork. No AI estimates. Just official government wage data from the BLS.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Browse jobs on Indeed, LinkedIn, or Glassdoor
2. SalaryLens automatically detects the job title and location
3. Salary percentile data (10th through 90th) appears right on the page
4. See instantly if the offer is below market, at market, or above
